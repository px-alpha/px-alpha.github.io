Books, articles and websites

Updated every now and then. An excerpt from my Firefox synced bookmarks collection.

Books

These books are currently on my reading table.

Software Wasteland: How the Application-Centric Mindset is Hobbling our Enterprises, by Dave McComb
An interesting read about the quagmire where enterprise IT is now, and the reasons why it is so complicated to get out of it.

Controlling the Chaos: A Functional Framework for Enterprise Architecture and Governance, by Robert Fox

Knowledge Graphs and Big Data Processing: by several authors. Download the free book here. A broad survey of state of the art methods and techniques in the area of knowledge graphs and big data. Quite recent, published in 2020.

Articles

gist: Buckets, Buckets Everywhere, Who Knows What to Think?
A Semantic Arts Article On Buckets, Collections and Categories, and the way they’re used in gist. The separation of the different kinds of buckets is important for everyone working on ontologies.

The intuitions behind Knowledge Graphs and Reasoning
An article by Peter Crocker, discussing reasoning approaches in rdf graphs (alike the rule approach I discussed in my article ‘A taste of GRAKN’). Peter discusses RDFox, one of the rule technologies avaialble for RDF.

The datacentric revolution
An article by Dave McComb, about data integration on similar, but not completely equivalant data sources. My upcoming article on RML.io contains a practical demonstration of the descibed ideas.

The 10 Vs of Big Data
An article by George Firican, about characteristics of data in 10 dimensions called the 10V’s. Insightful for those who need to explain data management approaches.

Websites

Foundations of Semantic Web Technologies from the TU Dresden
Follow this link and open the Dates and Materials tab. An excellent introduction in semantic technologies.

Euclid
the educational curriculum for the usage of linked data. This website is an excellent source of information in regard to linked data usage. Download the free book, or browse the information online.
